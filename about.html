The Cupcake Cafe & Catering

About Us

The Cupcake Cafe & Catering is not only a dessert company but also a catering company that puts an emphasis on quality and taste. We specialize in mobile full service catering. Since opening in 2010 Cupcake Cafe & Catering has been consistantly committed to progressing and maintaining the highest quality food and service to our customers.

Yevetta Bright: Founder

"I've always enjoyed cooking for others. Whether it was a home-cooked meal for my family or baked goods for my co-workers and friends. I love experimenting with new recipes and equally enjoyed sharing that deliciousness with them. After recieving encouragement from my son to start selling my kitchen creations- The Cupcake cafe was born. In 2015 as my passion and the demand for my catering expanded into new territories, I had to combine two businesses into one, which spawned the creation of The Cupcake Cafe and Catering".
